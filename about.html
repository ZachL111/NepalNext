About NepalNext

Our Mission

At NepalNext, we believe that the future of Nepal lies in the hands of its youth. Our mission is to provide a platform for young people, aged 13-19, to voice their ideas and contribute to the development of the nation. We aim to bridge the gap between the innovative ideas of the youth and the decision-makers in Nepal's government and corporate sectors.

What We Do

We provide an avenue for young visionaries to submit their ideas in various formats, including videos, PDFs, documents, and slideshows. These submissions are then curated and presented to leaders and authorities who have the power to implement them. We believe that by empowering the youth, we can collectively build a better future for Nepal.

Our Vision

We envision a Nepal where every young person has the opportunity to be heard and to make a meaningful impact. We strive to create a culture of innovation, leadership, and civic engagement, where the best ideas are recognized and transformed into reality.
